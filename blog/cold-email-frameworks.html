Cold Email Frameworks That Convert

Want more replies from your cold outreach? Use these proven frameworks to capture attention and earn meetings:

1. Problem–Insight–Action (PIA)

Problem: "Most reps waste hours chasing dead leads."
Insight: "We filter them with a custom AI signal platform."
Action: "Want a demo this week?"

2. 3 Value Bullets

“We recently helped:
✔ A SaaS brand cut churn 28%
✔ A fintech 3x email replies
✔ A team of 5 reps close $1.4M faster
Want to see if we can do the same?”

3. Question Hook

“How much revenue did your last cold email campaign drive?”
Then offer a compelling answer and CTA.
