Linkedln Organic Plus Social Selling

LinkedIn Organic + Social Selling 2.0 Toolkit (2025 Edition)

Generate high-quality inbound and outbound B2B leads using your personal brand, conversation frameworks, and AI-powered content.

TOOL COMPONENTS

1. Magnetized LinkedIn Profile (Built for Leads)

Your profile should convert visitors into leads, not just describe your job.

Checklist:

Headline = “I help [target audience] achieve [specific result] with [your solution]”

Featured section = Lead magnet, case study, or calendar link

About = Story-driven, value-packed, ends with a CTA (DM, book a call)

Services = Add keywords you want to rank for

Custom banner = With positioning, offer, and CTA

2. Social Selling Content System

Post 3–4x/week to build trust, visibility, and inbound DMs.

Content Pillars (rotate weekly):

Pain Point > Value Tip > CTA (e.g., carousel or short-form)

Thought Leadership (e.g., trends, contrarian takes)

Wins/Results/Case Studies (proof-driven)

Personal Story or “behind the scenes” (relatable)

Engagement Posts (polls, questions, “agree/disagree?”)

AI Tip: Use ChatGPT or Typeframes to turn 1 insight into 3–5 post formats.

3. DM Funnel (Inbound & Outbound)

Build relationships through genuine conversations, not cold pitches.

Inbound Strategy:

Like & reply to comments.

DM: “Hey [Name], thanks for engaging! Curious—what resonated with you most?”

Outbound Strategy (Social Selling 2.0):

Step 1: Engage with target profiles for 3–5 days (comments > likes)

Step 2: Send a non-pitch connection request

“Hey [Name], I liked your post on [X topic]. Thought it’d be great to connect!”

Step 3: Start a real convo after connection:

“Curious—what are you focused on in Q2 around [topic]?”

Step 4: Ask permission to share value:

“Would it be useful if I sent you a short guide we use with our clients on this?”

4. Comment-to-Lead Framework

Convert your best-performing posts into a lead magnet funnel.

How:

CTA: “Comment ‘Toolkit’ and I’ll send you the full system.”

Save commenter list.

DM each one:

“Thanks for commenting! Here’s the free guide I promised. Let me know what you think.” (Add link)

Bonus: Add them to your CRM and tag as “Engaged via Organic”.

5. Lead Magnet Distribution Engine

Push your lead magnet to build pipeline without paid ads.

Post once a week with a lead magnet CTA

Use your LinkedIn newsletter as a nurture sequence

Add each lead to your email CRM (e.g., ConvertKit, MailerLite)

Track clicks and replies with tools like Taplio or Shield

6. Conversion Event Funnel

Bring leads off LinkedIn into booked calls.

Examples:

Free workshop: “3 Strategies to Get 15+ B2B Leads/Week via LinkedIn”

Calendar CTA: “If you want help mapping this to your business, I’ll do it free on a 15-min call. Here’s my link.”
