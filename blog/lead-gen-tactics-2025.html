Top 5 Lead Gen Tactics That Still Work in 2025

While AI and automation tools have evolved, the fundamentals of great lead generation haven’t changed. Here are the top tactics still winning in 2025:

  1. Intent-based outreach: Target leads actively researching your category.
  2. Cold email with retargeting: Combine email outreach with remarketing ads for higher conversion.
  3. Lead magnets: Provide valuable content (e.g., calculators, playbooks) to drive opt-ins.
  4. Referral loops: Ask satisfied customers for intros or testimonials.
  5. AI-enhanced follow-up: Use tools like BD Agency’s AI Caller to automate voice-based follow-ups.

Stick to what works, but layer in modern tools for scale and speed.
