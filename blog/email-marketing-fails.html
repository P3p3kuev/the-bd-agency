Why Email Marketing Fails (And How to Fix It)

Email remains one of the highest ROI channels in B2B marketing, yet so many campaigns underperform. Here's why:

  • Spray-and-pray tactics: Sending mass emails without segmentation or personalization.
  • Lack of value: Messages that don't educate, entertain, or inform get ignored.
  • Poor subject lines: If your emails aren’t opened, nothing else matters.
  • Design overkill: Overuse of images and links gets flagged as spam.

The Fix: Build strong ICP profiles, personalize your outreach, test subject lines rigorously, and don’t forget to follow up. Consistency wins.
