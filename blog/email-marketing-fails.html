Why Email Marketing Fails (And How to Fix It)

Email marketing fails when it’s generic, untargeted, or robotic. Most B2B teams send one-size-fits-all messages to 1,000+ prospects and expect qualified meetings in return. Buyers expect relevance, context, and personalization.

Common Reasons Email Campaigns Fail

  • No personalization: using first name only is not enough
  • Overused templates or “salesy” language
  • No clear value or next step

How to Fix Your Campaigns

  • Use intent-based triggers and LinkedIn insights
  • Structure emails with proven frameworks (Problem–Solution–CTA)
  • Follow up 3–5x with varied messaging (e.g., value drop, testimonial)
