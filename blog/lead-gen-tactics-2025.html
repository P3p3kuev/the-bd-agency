Top 5 Lead Gen Tactics That Still Work

After thousands of outreach campaigns and dozens of industries tested, these are the five tactics that consistently bring results in 2025:

  • Intent-Based Prospecting: Use buyer intent signals from platforms like G2, Bombora, or LinkedIn activity to find active buyers.
  • AI-Personalized Outreach: Combine tools like Clay + GPT to generate high-context messages at scale.
  • LinkedIn + Email Sequencing: Start with a soft touch on LinkedIn, then follow up with a relevant email sequence.
  • Founder-to-Founder Messaging: Works especially well for early-stage startups selling to execs — use casual, direct tone.
  • Value-Packed Cold Emails: Offer something useful right away — an insight, tool, or idea — before asking for time.

Success lies in combining these tactics with constant A/B testing, relevance, and follow-up discipline.
