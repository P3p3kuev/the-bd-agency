Top 5 Lead Gen Tactics That Still Work

Despite the evolution of B2B sales, these 5 tactics continue to generate qualified leads and real pipeline in 2025:

  • Intent Data: Target buyers who are actively researching solutions like yours.
  • AI-Personalized Outreach: Use tools like ChatGPT to tailor cold emails at scale.
  • Multi-Channel Sequences: Combine email, LinkedIn, and phone for better response rates.
  • Video Intros: Loom-style intros that build trust and increase reply rates.
  • Live Chat SDRs: Use real reps (or AI ones) on your site to convert interest instantly.
